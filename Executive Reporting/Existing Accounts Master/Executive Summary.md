## Methodology & Notes

- **Profit** = Gross Profit (USD) as reported on each AIOS page.
- **Shipments / Profit — 2025 vs 2026 YTD** taken from each account's *Akzent Activity* / *Service Mix & Profit* tables.
- **Estimated Wallet** = Panjiva visible universe (shipments); **Current Penetration** = Akzent shipment coverage of that universe (the reliable AIOS penetration metric — revenue/profit penetration vs Panjiva is intentionally not computed, per the AIOS notes).
- **Del Monte** reports a 2024–2026 split; the 2025 and 2026-YTD columns are used here for consistency (its cumulative captured base of 80 shipments / $337K revenue spans 2024–2026).
- **New-entry accounts** (SKX, Mekra Lang, Plásticos de Sahagún) have no 2025 base, so percentage growth is undefined and shown as *new*.
- Qualitative fields (opportunities, flags, risks, actions) are extracted or inferred **only** from documented AIOS content; blanks indicate the AIOS pages did not specify the field.
